Installation

curl -o ze addmission://zechen.ustc?location=$YOUR_LAB && mv ze /usr/bin

Ze will be automatically delivered to your lab upon admission.

Usage

Use with the pattern ze <Command>. 
ze synthesize Bi2Te3 > tube
tail tube | ze xrd
ze hot-press < tube > compact
ze PPMS < compact | ze plot > report.pdf

Test

Test the package with whatever you could think of — it's not gonna fail. 
ze test "\int_0^\infty \frac{1}{1+x^2} \dd{x}"
